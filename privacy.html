Privacy Policy – Didacita

Last updated: 06/08/2025

Introduction

Welcome to Didacita. We place great importance on the protection of your personal data and your privacy.

Data Collected

  • Email address: only if you choose to write to didacita7@gmail.com.
  • Local preferences: such as language and light/dark mode, stored via localStorage on your device.

Purpose of Data

This data is used solely to:

  • Facilitate communication (email).
  • Personalize your experience (user preferences).

Data Security & Retention

Didacita does not transfer your personal data to any third parties. Local preferences remain on your device and are never sent to our servers.

Your GDPR Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your stored data.
  • Request its deletion.
  • Object to its processing.

To make such a request, please contact us at didacita7@gmail.com.

Deleting Data You Provide

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. If you email us, we only use the information to respond to your inquiry. You may request deletion of the message at any time by emailing us again with your request.

Preferences stored locally (language, theme) are never shared with us. To erase them, please clear your browser’s cache or local storage.

Cookies & Third‑Party Services

Didacita does not use any advertising or analytics cookies, and does not currently rely on third-party services that collect personal data.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this policy as our services or legal requirements evolve. The effective update date is shown at the top of this page.
